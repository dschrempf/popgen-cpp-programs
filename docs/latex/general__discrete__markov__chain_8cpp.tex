\subsection{Detailed Description}
Simulate a general discrete Markov chain with a given transition probability matrix P. 

\begin{DoxyAuthor}{Author}
Dominik Schrempf \href{mailto:dominik.schrempf@gmail.com}{\tt dominik.\+schrempf@gmail.\+com} 
\end{DoxyAuthor}
\begin{DoxyDate}{Date}
Wed Mar 18 15\+:51\+:19 2015
\end{DoxyDate}
Assume that P has a stationary distribution pi. Iterate the chain and print the proportion of time spent in each state. This should match the stationary distribution.
