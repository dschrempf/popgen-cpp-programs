Solution in Feres, homework 6.

N gas particles move around in a box that is divided into two symmetrical parts A (i particles) and B (N-i particles). A hole lets particles move from A to B and vice versa. If we neglect two particles passing at once, this model behaves like a Markov chain. Here, we simulate this chain.
