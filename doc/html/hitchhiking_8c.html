Detailed Description

Simulate hitchhiking along a positively selected locus.

Author
Dominik Schrempf domin.nosp@m.ik.s.nosp@m.chrem.nosp@m.pf@g.nosp@m.mail..nosp@m.com
Date
Wed Feb 11 14:31:40 2015

Gillespie - Population Genetics p. 110 Problem 4.3

Write a computer simulation to simulate the hitchhiking along a positively selected locus and look at the heterozygousity near this locus.

The parameters are:

  • \(p_a\) frequency of \(A1\)
  • \(q_a\) frequency of \(A2\)
  • \(p_b\) frequency of \(B1\)
  • \(q_b\) frequency of \(B2\)
  • \(x_1\) frequency of \(A_1B_1\)
  • \(x_2\) frequency of \(A_1B_2\)
  • \(x_3\) frequency of \(A_2B_1\)
  • \(x_4\) frequency of \(A_2B_2\)

The linkage disequlibrium is:

  • \(D = x_1 - p_A * p_B\)
