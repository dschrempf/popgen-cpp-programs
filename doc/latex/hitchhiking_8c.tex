\subsection{Detailed Description}
Simulate hitchhiking along a positively selected locus. 

\begin{DoxyAuthor}{Author}
Dominik Schrempf \href{mailto:dominik.schrempf@gmail.com}{\tt dominik.\+schrempf@gmail.\+com} 
\end{DoxyAuthor}
\begin{DoxyDate}{Date}
Wed Feb 11 14\+:31\+:40 2015
\end{DoxyDate}
Gillespie -\/ Population Genetics p. 110 Problem 4.\+3

Write a computer simulation to simulate the hitchhiking along a positively selected locus and look at the heterozygousity near this locus.

The parameters are\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item $p_a$ frequency of $A1$
\item $q_a$ frequency of $A2$
\item $p_b$ frequency of $B1$
\item $q_b$ frequency of $B2$
\item $x_1$ frequency of $A_1B_1$
\item $x_2$ frequency of $A_1B_2$
\item $x_3$ frequency of $A_2B_1$
\item $x_4$ frequency of $A_2B_2$
\end{DoxyItemize}

The linkage disequlibrium is\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item $D = x_1 - p_A * p_B$ 
\end{DoxyItemize}
